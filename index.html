About Us

Boompanda is a new-gen Gig platform exclusively for students on a mission to make financially independent, creative and skilled individuals among 200 million young Indian students by performing easy gigs and earning cash in rewards. We are India's Coolest Student Community with opportunities to socialize & network among other students across the nation through various events, trips, competitions, workshops, on-ground learning activities, etc. Along with providing various learning and earning opportunities.

बाप - Boom Army Program is a nationwide Ambassador program across colleges in India. Boom Army Program is a student-brand engagement program for the students who display बाप qualities. The program’s aim is to help you learn & understand how a brand management & promotion ecosystem in a campus operates.

So what are you waiting for? Be the imminent part of India’s Coolest Students Community.

What’s the Mission of a बाप

Stand for BoomPanda and help spread our word, market your favourite brands and expand our BoomPanda Community in your campus and city.

Also, don’t forget to make friends and have the most memorable experience!

What’s the Role of a बाप ?

Represent the Boom community among your network and campus.
Promote various brands on your campus and network.
Create fun memories along with various learning & earning opportunities.
Manage Talks, Workshops, and events on your campuses.
Align Boompanda with your community network by using marketing strategies and becoming a trendsetter.
Make our community grow across the cty.
Join our community of like-minded boomers who identify the latest trends in the market and make ideas into reality.

Who can be a बाप ?

Currently studying in 2nd, 3rd or 4th Year of College

Is a Team Player

Has great Leadership skills

Wants to be a Trendsetter

Enthusiastic for new things

Journey

Get ready to be a part of बाप

Legend Boomer

9 - 12 Months

  • Mandatory Brand Tasks

  • Mandatory Vertical Tasks

  • Completion Certificates from Brands (if any)

  • On-ground Meetups & Activities

  • Free Access to Events (First come first basis and subject to availability)

  • BoomPanda Legend Internship Certificate

  • Chance to get promoted as City Head or Zonal Head

  • Eligible for onsite trip

  • Recognition on Social Media and Website for top performers

  • Get a Minimum of 8000 BoomCoins(10 Boomcoins= Rs.1) in your wallet by performing various tasks to qualify for the next season.

Pro Boomer

6 - 9 Months

  • Exclusive Kits

  • Mandatory Brand Tasks

  • Mandatory Vertical Tasks

  • Completion Certificates from Brands (if any)

  • On-ground Meetups & Activities

  • Free Access to Events (First come first basis and subject to availability)

  • BoomPanda Pro Internship Certificate

  • Internship Opportunities from reputed organisations

  • Chance to get promoted as Team Lead

  • Recognition on Social Media and Website for top performers

  • Get a Minimum of 7000 BoomCoins(10 Boomcoins= Rs.1) in your wallet by performing various tasks to qualify for the next level.

Fire Boomer

3 - 6 Months

  • Receive a बाप Goodie

  • Mandatory Brand Tasks

  • Mandatory Vertical Tasks

  • Completion Certificates from Brands (if any)

  • On-ground Meetups & Activities

  • Free Access to Events (First come first basis and subject to availability)

  • BoomPanda Fire Internship Certificate

  • Internship Opportunities from reputed organisations

  • Recognition on Social Media and Website for top performers

  • Get a Minimum of 6000 BoomCoins(10 Boomcoins= Rs.1) in your wallet by performing various tasks to qualify for the next level.

Lit Boomer

1 - 3 Months

  • Accomplish mandatory Brand Tasks

  • Accomplish mandatory Vertical Tasks

  • Completion Certificates from Brands (if any)

  • BoomPanda Lit Internship Certificate

  • Recognition on Social Media and Website for top performers

  • Get a Minimum of 5000 BoomCoins(10 Boomcoins= Rs.1) in your wallet by performing various tasks to qualify for the next level.
